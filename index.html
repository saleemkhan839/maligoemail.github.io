Home financing process
Buying a home and obtaining a mortgage can be a lengty process, but there's no reason to get overwhelmed. We strive to make our loan process as easy and stress free as possible. With our informative loan process guide, we're outlining everything step-by-step.
PRE-APPROVAL PROCESS:
01
We collect the neccacerray documents for your pre-approval.
02
We meet with you and determine the best loan structure based on your financial goals and needs.
03
We issue your formal pre-approval. You begin making offers on homes with your real estate agent.
04
Once an offer is accepted, you're officially in contract. A closing date and terms of the contract are set.
